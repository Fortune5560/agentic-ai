Blueprint #2: Research & Reporting Agent

Goal: Produce trusted, executive-ready insights.

  • Autonomous source discovery
  • Credibility filtering
  • Verification loops
  • Human review before delivery

Outcome: Faster, higher-quality research.

Problem

Research is time-intensive, inconsistent, and difficult to verify at scale. Autonomous research without validation leads to hallucinations and low trust.

Goal

Build an agent that gathers information autonomously while enforcing verification, transparency, and human review.

System Design

  • Autonomous source discovery
  • Credibility filtering and ranking
  • Multi-step reasoning and synthesis
  • Explicit verification loops

Tooling

  • Retrieval: Search APIs, vector databases
  • Reasoning: LLM with structured prompts
  • Output: Reports, summaries, citations

Human-in-the-Loop

Final reports are reviewed and approved by a human before distribution or publication.

Outcome

High-quality, trustworthy research delivered faster, with full transparency into sources and reasoning.
